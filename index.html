Research

Our group focusses on understanding how humans perceive and interact with digital information, and to build technology that goes beyond the flat displays of PCs and smartphones to advances our capabilities when interacting with the digital world. To achieve this, we create and study enabling technologies and computational approaches that control when, where and how virtual content is displayed to increase the usability of AR and VR interfaces.
