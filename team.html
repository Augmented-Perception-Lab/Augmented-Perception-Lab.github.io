Work with us!

If you are interested in joining the group, please get in touch by sending an email to davidlindlbauer@cmu.edu. We are always looking for new team members that bring a diverse set of skills and motivations.

We offer positions for independent study projects, internships, research assistants, and postdocs. If you are interested in a PhD position at HCII and in the lab, check out the CMU HCII PhD admissions information, and reach out before applying.
